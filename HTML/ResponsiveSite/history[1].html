Enter Sandman

Listen to Enter Sandman on Spotify

or on YouTube

Before every football game and basketball game, Lane Stadium and Cassel Coliseum play Metallica's "Enter Sandman" while the fans jump up and down. At football games, this many times causes low grade earthquakes that can be read on seismic scales throughout the state of Virginia.



Skipper

For years, Virginia Military Institute (VMI) would fire off a cannon at their football games against VT, and chant "Where's your cannon?" Virginia Tech cadets responded by using brass from other cadets and some other metal they could find to build thier own cannon, named Skipper. They first fired Skipper when they played VMI, and tripled the charge to blow their hats off and shake windows. Now, Skipper is fired at every home game and other events.



Hokie Stone

Every new building on campus is built with VT's classic Hokie Stone. The Hokie Stone is mined from local Virginia mines by Virginia Tech mining students, and whenever a new building is constructed, Virginia Tech keeps the same architecture, making the Hokie Stone iconic.



There are many more traditions on campus too. Visit Blacksburg to experience these, and more for yourself!
